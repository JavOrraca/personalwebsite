Artificial Intelligence for Social Good


This is a great, 22-minute presentation by Neil Sahota at an AI conference. I met Sahota through UC Irvine, he recently taught my Technology & Analytics Consulting graduate course. Congrats, Professor Sahota! Keep up the momentum!
